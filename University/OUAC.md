## Group A or Group B applicant.
- The undergraduate application will automatically put you in the right group.
- Program selection, admission requirements, and deadlines may vary, depending on whether you are a group A or group B applicant.
### Group A applicant
You are a Group A applicant if **ALL** of the following are true:
- Currently taking 1 or more courses at an Ontario high school
- Are under 21 on or before December 31st, 2025
- Have an OSSD or are working toward an OSSD with 6 4U/M courses

### Group B applicant
You are a Group B applicant if you do not meet the group A criteria.
