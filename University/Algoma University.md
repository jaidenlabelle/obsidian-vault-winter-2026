- Canadian public university in the province of Ontario.
- Campuses in Brampton, Sault Ste. Marie, and Timmins.
- Specializes in liberal arts, sciences, management and professional degree programs.
- Founded in 1965.

## Problems
- Previously a residential school.
	- Unmarked graves on campus.
- Mostly international students.
